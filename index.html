Too Fat for Summer is an Arizona coverband; they cover coverable songs so people in the crowd have something they can sing along with as they get drunk. Knowing words to old songs makes bar attendees feel successful and more attractive because they are finally nailing a single thing in their sad life, a life in which they have lost all control.
